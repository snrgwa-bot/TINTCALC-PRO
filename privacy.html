Privacy Policy

TintCalc Pro - Window Film Estimator

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

Summary: TintCalc Pro respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

TintCalc Pro is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we collect:

  • Estimate Data: Customer names, contact info, and measurements you enter are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences and custom pricing are stored locally.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): If enabled, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account.
  • Subscription Info: Apple handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the app's core functionality (creating estimates)
  • Sync your data across your devices (if iCloud is enabled)
  • Verify your subscription status

3. Data Storage & Security

All your estimates and settings are stored:

  • Locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • In iCloud (optional) using Apple's encrypted cloud infrastructure

We do not have access to your data. Your information never passes through our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

TintCalc Pro uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management and payments
  • Apple iCloud: For optional data synchronization

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:
  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Track your location
  • Collect analytics about your usage

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Export your data at any time
  • Delete: Delete all data from Settings ‚Üí Data Management
  • Portability: Export and import your data freely

7. Children's Privacy

TintCalc Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
